The Journey

Founded in 2025 by a group of students who saw the need for a modern, student-centric approach to exam prep, MindForge was born. We started with a simple vision: to replace generic, one-size-fits-all study guides with dynamic, adaptive learning experiences. Our journey has taken us from a small test to a community of successful students. Every feature, from our custom practice tests, is built on the foundation of proven pedagogical science and real-world student feedback.
